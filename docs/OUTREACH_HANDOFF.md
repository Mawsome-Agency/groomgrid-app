## ✅ What Works Great to Demo

### 1. Signup Flow (15 seconds, clean)
- Email/password form
- Business name selection
- Instant dashboard access
- No card required for 14-day trial

### 2. Dashboard
- Clean, mobile-friendly UI
- Upcoming appointments at a glance
- Quick action buttons (New Client, New Appointment)
- Revenue summary (daily/weekly/monthly)

### 3. Booking Modal (The "Wow" Moment)
- 4 pre-configured services with prices:
  - Full Groom ($70, 60 min)
  - Bath & Brush ($45, 45 min)
  - Nail Trim ($20, 15 min)
  - De-shedding ($55, 50 min)
- Time slot selector (visual calendar)
- Add notes for the groomer
- Instant appointment creation

### 4. Client Profiles
- Client name, email, phone
- Pet name, breed, age
- Notes field (special instructions, behavior, allergies)
- Appointment history view

### 5. Calendar View
- Day/week/month toggle
- Color-coded appointments by service type
- Click to view/edit appointments
- Export option (Google Calendar sync coming soon)
