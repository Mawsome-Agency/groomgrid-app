## Usage Guidelines

### Reddit Engagement Rules (IMPORTANT)
- **Never post more than 1x per week** in r/doggrooming to avoid spam flags
- **Engage genuinely 5-10x** (helpful comments, no links) before posting anything with a CTA
- **Never use identical text** across subreddits — always vary the framing
- **Monitor thread responses** and reply authentically to every comment
- **If downvoted, don't repost** the same angle — try a different approach next time

### Facebook Group Rules
- **Check group rules** before posting — many prohibit self-promotion on certain days
- **Lead with value** — share a tip or story before mentioning the product
- **Respond to every comment** on your posts — engagement boosts visibility
- **Post during peak hours:** 7-8am and 6-8pm (groomers checking before/after their day)
- **Use grooming-specific language:** "groom," "finished dog," "van day," "bather" — signals insider status

### Tracking Notes
- All CTAs use `?coupon=BETA50` for attribution — monitor signup source in analytics
- Track which posts drive the most signups by noting post date + platform
- If a post gets 0 engagement after 48hrs, revise the hook and repost after 1 week
- Successful posts can be recycled every 6-8 weeks with updated framing
