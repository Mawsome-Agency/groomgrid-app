## Getting Started with GroomGrid

Welcome! You're one of the first groomers to use GroomGrid. This guide will get you set up in under 10 minutes.

### Step 1: Log In (1 minute)

1. Go to [getgroomgrid.com/login](https://getgroomgrid.com/login)
2. Enter the email you signed up with
3. Check your inbox for the verification email
4. Click the link to verify and set your password

**Troubleshooting:**
- No verification email? Check your spam folder
- Link expired? Request a new one on the login page
- Still stuck? Email sofia@getgroomgrid.com

### Step 2: Add Your First Client (2 minutes)

Click **"Add Client"** and fill in:
- **Owner name** — e.g., "Sarah Mitchell"
- **Phone number** — for SMS reminders
- **Email** — optional, for appointment confirmations

Then add their pet:
- **Pet name** — e.g., "Biscuit"
- **Breed** — e.g., "Goldendoodle"
- **Breed-specific notes** — e.g., "Nervous with nail trims, needs muzzle for full groom"
- **Vaccination status** — record what's current

**Pro tip:** Use the breed notes for anything you'd normally keep in your head. This is your safety net — every detail you add now saves you time later.

### Step 3: Book an Appointment (2 minutes)

1. Click **"New Appointment"**
2. Select the client + pet
3. Choose the service type (full groom, bath + brush, nail trim, etc.)
4. Set the date and time
5. Enable **SMS Reminder** (on by default)

**What the client sees:** They get a text 24 hours before their appointment. No more no-shows eating into your gas money.

### Step 4: Explore Payments (2 minutes)

1. Go to **Settings → Payments**
2. Connect your Stripe account (or skip for now)
3. When ready, you can:
   - Send invoices after grooming
   - Accept credit card payments in the app
   - Set up recurring billing for regular clients

**Note:** Payment setup is optional during beta. You can add it anytime.

### Step 5: Check Your Dashboard (1 minute)

Your dashboard shows:
- **Today's appointments** at a glance
- **Upcoming this week** with client details
- **Revenue tracking** (when payments are enabled)
- **Client activity** — who's booked, who's overdue for a groom

## Mobile Groomer Quick Start

If you groom in a van or travel to clients:

1. **Add client addresses** — we use these for route planning (coming soon!)
2. **Set mobile service area** — in Settings, define your service radius
3. **SMS reminders are your best friend** — mobile groomers lose $50-100 in gas per no-show. Reminders cut no-shows by 30%+.

## Salon Owner Quick Start

If you manage a team:

1. **Add your groomers** — in Settings → Team, invite your staff
2. **Assign clients** — each client can have a preferred groomer
3. **View team schedule** — see everyone's appointments on one calendar
4. **Staff-level reporting** — track each groomer's bookings and revenue

## Cat Groomer Quick Start

If you specialize in cats:

1. **Use temperament tags** — add "cat-specific" behavior notes (stress level, handling preferences)
2. **Set longer appointment slots** — cat grooms typically need 1.5-2 hours
3. **Add vaccination/health notes** — track FeLV/FIV status, sedation history
4. **Custom service types** — set up cat-specific services (lion cut, sanitary clip, etc.)
