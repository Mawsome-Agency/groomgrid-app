# GroomGrid Beta Weekly Pulse Survey

**Platform:** Google Forms (link: [TO BE CREATED])
**Frequency:** Weekly (sent every Monday)
**Time to complete:** 5 minutes
**Audience:** Founding Groomer beta testers

---

## Survey Questions

### Question 1: What's one thing GroomGrid did well for you this week?

**Type:** Long text (paragraph)
**Required:** Yes
**Help text:** Think about a specific moment where GroomGrid saved you time, prevented a problem, or just worked smoothly.

### Question 2: What's one thing that confused you or didn't work?

**Type:** Long text (paragraph)
**Required:** Yes
**Help text:** Be honest! This is the most valuable feedback we can get. Bugs, confusing UI, missing features — tell us everything.

### Question 3: On a scale of 1-10, how likely are you to keep using GroomGrid after the beta?

**Type:** Linear scale (1 = Definitely Not, 10 = Definitely Yes)
**Required:** Yes
**Help text:** Think about this honestly. If your answer is below 7, we'd love to know why in the comments.

---

## Optional Follow-up (shown if score ≤ 6)

### What would need to change for you to rate GroomGrid a 9 or 10?

**Type:** Long text (paragraph)
**Required:** No

---

## End-of-Beta Interview Questions (30 min call)

### Warm-up (2 min)
1. Tell me about your grooming business — how long, what type, how many clients?
2. What tools were you using before GroomGrid? (Paper, Google Calendar, MoeGo, etc.)

### Product Experience (15 min)
3. Walk me through your last week using GroomGrid. When did you open it? What did you do?
4. What's the ONE feature you can't live without now?
5. What's the biggest thing missing that would make you switch from [current tool]?
6. How many clients did you add this week? How many appointments did you book?
7. Did any clients comment on the SMS reminders? What did they say?
8. Were there any moments where you went "this is exactly what I needed"? Tell me about them.
9. Were there any moments where you went "this is broken/missing"? Tell me about them.

### Willingness to Pay (8 min)
10. If GroomGrid cost $29/month, would you pay for it? Why or why not?
11. What would make it worth $29/month for you? (What features would need to be there?)
12. How does $14.50/month (founding groomer rate) feel? Fair? Too much? Too little?
13. Would you recommend GroomGrid to another groomer right now? What would you say?

### Feature Prioritization (5 min)
14. Rank these features from most to least important to you:
    - [ ] Automated SMS/email reminders
    - [ ] Pet profiles with breed/behavior notes
    - [ ] Online booking page for clients
    - [ ] Payment processing (credit cards)
    - [ ] Revenue reporting
    - [ ] Route optimization (for mobile groomers)
    - [ ] Team scheduling (for salons)
    - [ ] Client retention tracking (who's overdue)
    - [ ] Grooming report cards for clients

### Closing (2 min)
15. Anything else you want to tell us?
16. Would you be willing to provide a testimonial if you're happy with GroomGrid?

## Creating the Google Form

**Setup steps:**
1. Go to [forms.google.com](https://forms.google.com)
2. Create new form: "GroomGrid Founding Groomer — Week {{N}} Pulse Survey"
3. Add the 3 questions above
4. Settings → Presentation → Show progress bar
5. Settings → Responses → Collect email addresses (required)
6. Settings → Responses → Limit to 1 response (yes)
7. Send link to testers via email each Monday
8. Create separate form for each week (Week 1, Week 2, Week 3, Week 4)

**Week naming convention:**
- Week 1 (May 8-14): Pulse Survey sent May 12
- Week 2 (May 15-21): Pulse Survey sent May 19
- Week 3 (May 22-28): Pulse Survey sent May 26
- Week 4 (May 29-Jun 4): Pulse Survey sent Jun 2
