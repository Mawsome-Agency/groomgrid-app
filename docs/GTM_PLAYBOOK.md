## Executive Summary

**The Opportunity:** Pet grooming software market is dominated by legacy players with no AI capabilities. Mobile groomers and small salons are underserved by enterprise pricing and complex systems.

**Our Edge:** AI-first, mobile-optimized, and priced for independents ($29-79/mo vs $49-149/mo).

**The Play:** Aggressive community-first launch leveraging trust in Facebook groomer groups, supplemented by TikTok/Instagram visibility, with SEO as a long-term foundation.

**Realistic Expectations:** To hit 100 subscribers in 24 days, we need ~4-5 paid conversions/day. With a 3-5% conversion from warm leads (group members who engage), we need ~100-150 warm leads/day. With 0.5-1% engagement in groups, we need presence in ~10-20 active groups posting daily.

### Program Structure

**Incentives:**
- You: $20 credit per referral who pays
- Referrer: 1 month free ($29-79 value) per signup

**Tiers:**
| Referrals | Bonus | Cumulative |
|-----------|-------|------------|
| 1-2 | 1 month free per signup | 1-2 months free |
| 3-5 | +$50 Amazon gift card | Up to $50 + months free |
| 6-10 | +Lifetime $19/mo pricing | Up to $100 + locked price |
| 10+ | Custom rewards | Partnership opportunities |

### Mechanics

**How It Works:**
1. User gets unique referral link after signup
2. Shares link with groomer friends
3. Friend signs up → Both get credit automatically
4. Credits apply immediately (no waiting)

**Tracking:**
- Unique referral code in user dashboard
- Real-time tracking of clicks, signups, conversions
- Monthly referral leaderboards in Facebook groups

- Success stories from top referrers

- Double time investment in top-performing channel

**If zero signups by April 10:**

- Deep dive on user feedback from demos
