## Engagement Rules

### DO:
✅ Like 10+ posts per day in each group
✅ Comment with value on 5+ posts per day
✅ Respond to every comment on your posts within 1 hour
✅ DM warm leads who engage with your content
✅ Be authentic and transparent
✅ Share real struggles and wins

### DON'T:
❌ Post more than once per day per group
❌ Sell directly in posts (save for DMs)
❌ Ignore comments or questions
❌ Be spammy or promotional
❌ Post the same content across all groups simultaneously (stagger by 2+ hours)
