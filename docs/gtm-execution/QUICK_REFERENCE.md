## Red Flags — When to Pause

### Immediate Action Required (Stop within 24 hours)

🚨 **Zero interview responses after 20 outreach messages**
- Action: Revise outreach template

🚨 **Facebook DM response rate < 10%**
- Action: Revise DM templates

🚨 **Reddit post gets < 5 upvotes after 24 hours**
- Action: Delete post, try different topic

🚨 **Zero signups after 7 days of active engagement**
- Action: Review all channels, test messaging

🚨 **Interviewees say pricing is way off**
- Action: Conduct pricing survey

### Warning Signs (Monitor Closely)

⚠️ **Interview no-show rate > 30%**
- Action: Improve reminder system

⚠️ **Single channel > 80% of engagement**
- Action: Diversify channels

⚠️ **Pain points don't match assumptions**
- Action: Adjust product roadmap

⚠️ **Engagement but zero signups**
- Action: Review conversion funnel

## Quick Decision Guide

### Should I post this?
- ✅ Yes: Provides value, shares experience, asks genuine question
- ❌ No: Direct pitch, same content across groups, spammy

### Should I DM this person?
- ✅ Yes: Engaged with your content, mentioned pain point, active contributor
- ❌ No: Random cold DM, no prior interaction, looks like spam

### Should I mention GroomGrid?
- ✅ Yes: In DMs after engagement, in response to direct questions, after 10+ helpful comments
- ❌ No: In first comments, in first posts, without prior value

### Should I follow up?
- ✅ Yes: After 2-3 days of no response, after demo, after interview
- ❌ No: Same day, more than 3 times, after they said no
