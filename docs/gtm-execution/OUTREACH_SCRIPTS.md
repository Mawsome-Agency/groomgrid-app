## Facebook Group Engagement Scripts

### Script 1: Value-Add Comment (No Pitch)

**When to use:** Commenting on other people's posts about scheduling, payments, or business challenges

```
Great question!

I've dealt with this too. What worked for me was [specific solution based on their post].

Here's what I do:
- [Step 1]
- [Step 2]
- [Step 3]

It took some trial and error, but now [result].

Anyone else have tips for this?
```

### Script 2: Pain Point Empathy Comment

**When to use:** Someone complains about no-shows, double bookings, or payment chasing

```
Ugh, I feel this! 

I used to lose [X amount] per week on [their pain point]. 

What finally worked for me was:
- [Solution 1]
- [Solution 2]

Cut my losses by [X%]. Still frustrating sometimes, but at least it doesn't ruin my whole day anymore.

What's your strategy for handling this?
```

### Script 3: Helpful Resource Comment

**When to use:** Someone asks for recommendations or resources

```
Great question!

For [topic], I'd recommend:
- [Resource 1] - because [reason]
- [Resource 2] - because [reason]

I've also been experimenting with [related thing] that's been working well for my grooming business.

Happy to share more details if you're interested!
```

## Interview Scripts

### Script 1: Interview Opening

```
Thanks so much for taking the time!

I really appreciate you sharing your experience with me. This is purely research — no sales pitch, I promise.

I want to understand what actually matters to groomers running their businesses day-to-day.

Ready when you are!
```

### Script 2: Pain Point Discovery

```
Let's start with your business — are you mobile, salon-based, or both?

[Wait for answer]

How many dogs do you typically groom per day?

[Wait for answer]

Now, walk me through your yesterday — from waking up to done with work. Where did you get frustrated?

[Listen and probe deeper]

When did you last think "there HAS to be a better way to do this" — what was it about?
```

### Script 3: Solution Validation

```
If software could fix ONE thing for you, what would you pay for?

[Wait for answer]

What would make you switch from your current setup?

[Wait for answer]

What made you choose your current software? What do you hate about it?

[Wait for answer]

Price check: At $29/month for scheduling + reminders + payments — does that feel too cheap, just right, or too expensive?

[Wait for answer]

At what price would it be a "no-brainer" for you?
```

### Script 4: Closing

```
This has been incredibly helpful — thank you so much!

Your insights are exactly what I needed to hear.

I'll send that $25 Amazon gift card within 24 hours.

If you think of anything else or want to try out what we're building, just let me know!

Best,
Sofia
```

## Quick Tips for All Scripts

### DO:
✅ Personalize every message (use their name, reference their comment)
✅ Keep it short and conversational
✅ Focus on value first, selling second
✅ Be authentic and transparent
✅ Respond to every reply within 24 hours

### DON'T:
❌ Copy-paste without personalization
❌ Be overly formal or corporate
❌ Push too hard for a sale
❌ Ignore responses
❌ Send the same message twice
