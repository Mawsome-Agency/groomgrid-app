## CTA Strategy by Funnel Stage

### Bottom Funnel (comparison, alternatives, "best X software")
**Goal**: Convert immediately
**CTA style**: Direct, benefit-specific, urgency
**Examples**:
- "Switch to GroomGrid — 50% off your first month"
- "See why groomers choose GroomGrid over MoeGo — try it free"
- "Start your free trial — no credit card required"

### Mid Funnel (pain point, operations, problem-solution)
**Goal**: Show value, then convert
**CTA style**: Problem-to-solution, feature-specific
**Examples**:
- "Stop losing $200/month to no-shows — set up automated reminders free"
- "Get paid at booking time — try GroomGrid free for 14 days"
- "Book more dogs, chase fewer invoices — start your free trial"

### Top Funnel (how-to, career, educational)
**Goal**: Capture interest, build trust
**CTA style**: Soft, educational, value-first
**Examples**:
- "See how GroomGrid helps new mobile groomers — free trial"
- "Plan your grooming business startup with GroomGrid"
- "Get the free mobile grooming checklist + try GroomGrid free"
